About

Creative Juniors is a concern of the Creative IT Institute. The organization has been providing IT training for 13 years. This time Creative IT institute takes a step forward for the juniors. The purpose of our unique platform is to enable our future generations to keep pace with the new era of technology. Where juniors can acquire the necessary up-to-date knowledge and build themselves up for a beautiful future.

The courses on the Creative Juniors platform are designed so that the subjects will not only be considered as a basic life skill for the juniors but will also prepare them for the new IT-centric or international careers that will emerge in the future world.

The appreciation will build their confidence. Top of that, these talented youngsters will also be able to participate in national and International Tech Competitions such as Doodle For Google, Scratch Olympiad, Code 4 fun etc.
